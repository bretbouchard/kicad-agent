<objective>
Generate 2000+ open-ended question-answer pairs from real schematics for fine-tuning circuit understanding models. Unlike PCB MMLU (multi-choice), this is a free-form QA dataset that teaches models to explain circuits, trace signals, diagnose violations, and suggest improvements.

Purpose: The PCB MMLU (Phase 41) measures understanding. This dataset *teaches* understanding. Fine-tuning on QA pairs improves the model's ability to generate explanations, not just pick correct answers. This is the training data that makes the benchmark scores go up.

Output: CircuitQAPair schemas, QA generator, and the canonical circuit-qa-v1.json dataset.
</objective>

               "component_function",
               "net_purpose",

           answer: str = Field(min_length=20, max_length=10000)
           source: str = Field(min_length=1, max_length=512)
