<objective>
Convert 136K correct maze reasoning chains to ChatML instruction-following format, score with the trained reward model, remove bottom quartile, and produce train/val/test JSONL splits for SFT training.

Purpose: Transform raw chain data into instruction-following format that Qwen2.5-1.5B-Instruct can learn from via supervised fine-tuning. Quality filtering ensures only high-quality reasoning chains are used for training.

Output: `training_output/sft_prepared/` with train.jsonl, val.jsonl, test.jsonl containing ChatML-formatted, quality-filtered samples.
</objective>

Scores each sample's `messages[-1]["content"]` (assistant response) with `predict_reward()`. Sorts by composite score `(format + quality + accuracy) / 3`. Keeps top `keep_fraction`. Sets `quality_score` on retained samples.
